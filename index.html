About Me.

Hi! I'm Cailey and I am a front-end developer based in Austin, TX.

I have always loved solving puzzles and creating things since I was a child (I was the kid in middle school who taught myself how to customize my MySpace profile through coding). My first formal introduction to coding was when I was in college, where I ultimately received a BS degree in Pure Mathematics. However, I found myself on a different path, where I have built up years of customer service experience in the restaurant industry.

Now I have reignited my passion for technology and web development; coding challenges me to think in new ways, both creatively and logically, while also pushing me to constantly learn new things. Ultimately, I hope to join a team where I can contribute my passion for building efficient, user-friendly websites, to make the web a little brighter together.

When I am not coding, I can be found daydreaming about my next travel adventure, practicing yoga, or getting lost in the rabbit hole of books and video games.
